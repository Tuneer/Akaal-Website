Private Security

The Guarding Division of AEPL is a professional body of Total Security Solutions. We provide innovative and strategic security services customized to minimize losses. The service portfolio of AEPL includes guarding, bodyguards for industries, banks, retail outlets, residential colonies, hotels and institutions. Incorporated in 2010, AEPL today has a presence in Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh & Maharashtra, backed by our trained security personnel maintaining prompt services, with emergency response, proactive operations and backup team and a dynamic corporate infrastructure at all branch locations. All our security guards are first verified by police. Before joining our company, they go thorough very meticulous training sessions so can perform all types of security relates tasks. Our first class guarding services are offered to provide protection commercial, industrial, residential, governmental and institutional assets. We select guards on basis of merit and after ensuring verification by local police. Our guards are trained to handle all types of emergencies in a best possible way. We also organize training sessions time after time in order to revive their capabilities. We also make our security personnel aware about the latest updates of technologies, security situations and procedures.
